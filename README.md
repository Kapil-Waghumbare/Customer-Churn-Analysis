# Customer Churn Analysis

## Overview
Analysis of 7,032 telecom customers to identify churn drivers 
and recommend retention strategies.

**Tools Used:** SQL (MySQL Workbench) · Power BI · DAX · Excel

---

## Problem Statement
The company has a churn rate of 26.58% — well above the 
industry average of 5–7%. This translates to $139,130 in 
monthly revenue loss ($1.67M annually).

---

## Key Findings
- Month-to-month customers churn at 42.71% vs 2.85% for two-year contracts
- 47.68% of customers who leave do so within the first 12 months
- Electronic check users churn at 45.29% vs ~15% for auto-payment users
- Churned customers have 40% lower lifetime value ($1,531 vs $2,555)

---

## Recommendations
1. Offer discounts to move customers to annual contracts
2. Improve onboarding experience in the first 12 months
3. Incentivise automatic payment setup
4. Flag and contact high-risk customers early
